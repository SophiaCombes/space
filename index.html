Welcome, Brave Pirate

Ye've discovered a legendary pirate map hidden on the campus. Follow the clues from port to port, and after five stops ye'll reach the final challenge where the booty awaits!

Yer mission: Sail through the riddles, choose the correct ports, and after five clues ye'll find the key to unlock the treasure chest. Claim yer riches!

How to Play:

  1. Set sail to each port in order
  2. Decipher the pirate riddle at that port
  3. Chart a course to find the next port of call
  4. Select the correct port to proceed
  5. Complete all 5 riddles to reach Davy Jones' treasure chest
