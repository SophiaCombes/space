RIDDLE #4

THE FOURTH CLUE

Where wheels turn and journeys begin,
Where travelers wait and paths spin,
Find the hub where routes connect,
Where destinations you can select.

⚓ Pirate's Clue:

This be the harbor where ships come and go, a crossroads of the seas. Search the docks. There be a clue that points ye onward, captain.
