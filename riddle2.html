RIDDLE #2

THE SECOND CLUE

Under the open sky, where green grass grows,
Where children play and the fresh air flows,
Find the spot where benches rest,
Where nature's beauty is at its best.

⚓ Pirate's Clue:

This port be outdoors, a place fer restin' and relaxation. Search the green seas of yer campus. Yer next map piece be hidden there.
