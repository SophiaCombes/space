THE THIRD CLUE

Where steam rises and aromas fill the air,
Where morning starts and people share,
Seek the place where coffee brews,
Where conversations and energy renews.

⚓ Pirate's Clue:

This be a warm port where crew gathers and grog be served. Seek the tavern where yer next clue awaits, matey.
